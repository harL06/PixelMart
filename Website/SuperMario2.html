Super Mario Bros. 2

Super Mario Bros. 2 is a 2D side-scrolling platform game. The objective is to navigate the player's character through the dream world Subcon and defeat the main antagonist Wart.

Before each stage, the player chooses one of four protagonists: Mario, Luigi, Toad, and Princess Toadstool.

All four characters can run, jump, and climb ladders or vines, but each character possesses a unique strength that causes them to be controlled differently.
