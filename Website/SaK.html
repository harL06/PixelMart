Sonic & Knuckles

Sonic & Knuckles is a 1994 platform video game developed and published by Sega. You can control Sonic the Hedgehog and Knuckles the Echidna in their quests to save Angel Island;

Sonic tries to prevent Doctor Robotnik from relaunching his orbital weapon, the Death Egg, while Knuckles scuffles with Robotnik's minion, EggRobo.

Like previous Sonic games, players traverse side-scrolling levels at high speeds while collecting rings and defeating enemies.
